## 1. WHY CHANGE THE MINDSET?

### The Old Way: "AI follows orders"

You have an idea â†’ You describe in detail â†’ AI designs accordingly â†’ AI codes

**Problems:**
- You must know everything beforehand (layout, colors, features...)
- AI only knows what you tell it, misses what you don't know to ask
- Result depends on your description ability

### The New Way: "AI & Human are Partners"

You state the goal â†’ AI proposes from experience â†’ You provide context â†’ Adjust together â†’ AI codes

**Benefits:**
- AI has "seen" millions of projects, knows effective patterns
- You only need to know the GOAL and CONTEXT
- Result is a combination: AI's expertise + your reality
